Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a waste complaint?

To submit a waste complaint, create an account or log in, navigate to the "File Complaint" section, fill out the required details about the waste issue including location and photos if available, and submit the form. You'll receive a confirmation and tracking ID for your complaint.

How long does it take to resolve a complaint?

Resolution time varies depending on the nature and complexity of the waste issue. Simple complaints may be resolved within 3-5 business days, while more complex issues might take 1-2 weeks. You can track the status of your complaint in real-time through your dashboard.
